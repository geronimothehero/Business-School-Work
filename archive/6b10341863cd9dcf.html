Company Profile

Pfizer, Inc. is the world's leading pharmaceutical group. Net sales break down by family of products as follows: - primary care products (47.4%): medicines used in internal medicine, vaccines, medicines for the prevention and treatment of Covid-19, antivirals, mRNA-based products, etc.; - specialty care products (26.2%): medicines used in immunology, in hospitals and for the treatment of rare diseases, anti-inflammatory medicines, etc.; - oncology products (24.5%); - other (1.9%). The United States accounts for 60.8% of net sales.
